About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Eindhoven University of Technology. My research focuses on the development of computer vision algorithms for the analysis of medical images. I am particularly interested in the application of deep learning techniques for the early diagnosis of cancer using 3D medical images. My research is part of a close collaboration between the Eindhoven University of Technology, the Erasmus University Medical Center Rotterdam, and the Catharina Hospital Eindhoven.
